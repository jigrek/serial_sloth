solana's slowest serial killer

Meet $SLOTHER, the Solana's slowest serial killer. With a murder rate that’s as lethargic as his lifestyle, he plans his crimes meticulously over months, maybe even years. Victims often escape purely because $SLOTHER hasn’t finished his dramatic killer monologue by the time they wake up. Beware: his deadly patience is matched only by his love for naptime!

Once a simple, slow-moving sloth named Sid, his life changed dramatically when a group of cheeky monkeys pranked him by painting him neon pink. Humiliated and mocked by the entire jungle, Sid vowed revenge, but in true sloth fashion, he took his sweet time plotting it out. Years later, Sid became the world's slowest serial killer, whose victims often had time to take a nap, read a book, and move to a different tree before he ever got around to finishing them off.
